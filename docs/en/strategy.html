Alpha-X7 is a trend-following strategy based on W/M pattern detection.

Key Features

  • üìà W Pattern (Double Bottom): Long entry signal
  • üìâ M Pattern (Double Top): Short entry signal
  • üîç RSI Filter: Confirms overbought/oversold conditions
  • üåê MTF (Multi-Timeframe): Higher timeframe trend alignment
  • üéöÔ∏è ATR-based SL/TP: Volatility-adjusted risk management
  • üîÑ Trailing Stop: Profit protection mechanism

üü¢ Entry Conditions

Long Entry (Buy)

Condition Criteria
Pattern W Pattern (Double Bottom) detected
RSI < 35 (Oversold condition)
MTF Trend Uptrend on higher timeframe
Validity Within configured time after pattern detection
When all conditions are met, entry occurs at the open of the next bar.

Condition Criteria
Pattern M Pattern (Double Top) detected
RSI > 65 (Overbought condition)
MTF Trend Downtrend on higher timeframe
Validity Within configured time after pattern detection

üìê W/M Pattern Explanation

W Pattern (Double Bottom)


         /\
        /  \    /
       /    \  /
      /      \/
     L1      L2

L1: First low point

L2: Second low point (similar or slightly higher than L1)

‚Üí Bullish reversal expected after L2 ‚Üí Long entry

M Pattern (Double Top)


      H1      H2
     /  \    /  \
    /    \  /    \
   /      \/      \

H1: First high point

H2: Second high point (similar or slightly lower than H1)

‚Üí Bearish reversal expected after H2 ‚Üí Short entry

üîÑ Trailing Stop

How It Works

  1. After entry, price moves in favorable direction
  2. When profit reaches trail_start_r √ó Risk, trailing starts
  3. As price continues favorably, SL moves along
  4. When price retraces by trail_dist_r √ó Risk, position closes

Example

Entry price: $100
SL: $95 (Risk = $5)
trail_start_r = 1.0
trail_dist_r = 0.2

Price rises to $105 (1R profit)
‚Üí Trailing starts
‚Üí New SL = $104 (High - 0.2R)

Price rises to $108
‚Üí New SL = $107

Price drops to $107
‚Üí SL hit, closed (Profit: $7)
